About Me

Hi, I am Surja Sanyal, currently working as a Doctoral Reserach Fellow in the department of Computer Science and Engineering of Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati, India, under the supervision of Dr. Manas Khatua, on Industrial IoT network security using machine learning methods. Prior to this, I have received my M.Tech. in the department of Computer Science and Engineering at National Institute of Technology Durgapur, India, under the supervision of Dr. Sajal Mukhopadhyay. During my M.Tech., I worked on Game Theoretic Settings for Surplus Food Distribution.

My current research interests include Machine Learning (ML) / Deep Learning (DL) based security of Internet of Things (IoT) networks and the application of Game Theory (GT) in social setups.
